ATTACK ON TITAN

Attack on Titan involves 2 kingdoms the Marley and the Eldia Empires the Marley who is at war with Eldia decides to attack the walls of Eldia by sending titans to attack the walls. By destroying the walls Eren Yeagar the protagonist of the series witnessed the destruction of his home and his mother getting eaten by a titan. After witnessing everything from his life get taken away from him he vowed revenge by using the Founding titan powers from his father he got his revenge by destroying Marley.



All Characters:

Levi Ackerman

Levi Ackerman is the best soldier of humanity he is the leader of his squad of scout regiment the "Levi Squad" and can defeat any titan without a flinch. His agility and power are no match for a titan. And even with titan like powers he can still retain his humanity.

Eren Yeagar

Eren Yeagar is a member of the scout regiment and he is a member of the Levi Squad he also holds the power of one of the founding titans from his father. He is one of the strongest people in both his human and titan form. Not only does he hold the power of the attack titan but 2 other founding titans as well with the ability to command titans and summon war hammers.

Mikasa Ackerman

Mikasa Ackerman is a second cousin of Levi Ackerman which means she has superhuman abilities just like levi while maintaining her humanity. She is very fast and powerful which matches Levi's power. She was also adopted by the father of Eren at a young age when her parents were murdered.

Armin Arlert

Armin Arlert is one of the close friends of Eren Yeagar and grew up with him. Armin is a part of the Levi Squad along with Mikasa and Eren. Armin also posseses the "Colossal Titan" after consuming the original Colossal Titan owner (Berdholdt) after a near death experience from the original colossal titan.

Grisha Yeagar

Grisha Yeagar is the father of Eren Yeagar and used to be a citizen of the Marley Empire until he decided to move to the Eldia Empire and start a new life. He got married and had a wife and became a doctor he was the original holder of the attack titan before Eren. He also used to hold the power of the founding titan by eating Frieda Reiss and stealing her power. Before dying he turned Eren into a titan and made him eat him so that Eren could hold the attack and founding titan powers.
